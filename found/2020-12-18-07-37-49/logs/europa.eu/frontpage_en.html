Welcome to EU and ME

#EUandME

What matters most to you? From beachcombing to backpacking, surfing the internet to saving the world, living in the European Union provides endless opportunities to do what you love – wherever that may lead. As a young citizen, the EU can give you all the tools you need to unlock your potential and ignite your inspiration. Whether you’re an energetic entrepreneur, a digital devotee or a protector of the planet, it all comes down to #EUandME. 

Get inspired by our #EUandME short films directed by renowned European filmmakers and learn more about the link between your pastime and the EU.
